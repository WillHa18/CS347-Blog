This video introduces a basic introduction to how web development works and gives various explanation on vocabulary, HTML, CSS, Javascript, etc. Roles in web development are filled in the following three categories: Front-End, Back-End, and Full-Stack. Front-End in web development are mostly what you can mostly see on your website, while the Back-End is the parts what you can't see such as the logics and functionality that is making the website allow to work properly. HTML contains all of the content that is currently on the webpage and is what the browser loads up when you visit a particular website. CSS helps customize all of the HTML contents into a design of your choosing, while Javascript adds functionality to inputs performed by the users within the website.
